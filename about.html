About Me

Christina Zaitsev

Christina Zaitsev is a professional ice skating coach with over 15 years of experience. She started skating at the age of 5 and has competed in numerous national and international competitions. Zaitsev has a passion for teaching and has helped many students achieve their goals, whether it’s landing their first jump or competing at the highest levels.

Teaching Philosophy

Zaitsev believes in a personalized approach to coaching, where each student’s unique strengths and weaknesses are addressed. Her goal is to foster a love for skating while developing the technical skills needed to excel.
