About Me

Christina Zaitsev

Christina Zaitsev is a professional ice skating coach with over 15 years of experience. She started skating at the age of 5 and has competed in numerous national and international competitions. Zaitsev has a passion for teaching and has helped many students achieve their goals, whether it’s landing their first jump or competing at the highest levels.

Achievements

  • National and International Competitor             2010-2014
  • U.S. Nationals Champion / Juvenile Pairs       2010
  • U.S. Nationals Medalist / Intermediate Pairs   2011
  • U.S. National Champion / Novice Pairs           2013
  • Team USA Member                                          2013-2014
  • ISU Junior Grand Prix, Croatia                         2013
  • ISU Junior Grand Prix, Belorussia                    2014
  • ISU Junior Grand Prix, Latvia                           2014

Teaching Philosophy

Zaitsev believes in a personalized approach to coaching, where each student’s unique strengths and weaknesses are addressed.
